Terminal Studio: Part 1

How can we create a healthier future for people and planet?

This project addresses concerns of health of people and planet with closed loop urban agriculture. This project specifically caters low income, youth demographics from single parent households along North Williams Avenue, using the philosophy of breaking generational cycles of health inequity.
