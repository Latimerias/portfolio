ABOUT



Hello! I am a designer with an interest in sustainability through the lens of ecology and decreased dependencies to create closed and self sustaining systems. Socially I am concerned with food sovereignty and the potential for permaculture practices in closed loop systems which plays hand in hand with ecological sustainability. I also have a strong passion for computer graphics and technology to develop more efficient workflows in the architectural pipeline especially between BIM and visualization.
