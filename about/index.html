ABOUT



Hello, I am a current architecture student with an interest in sustainability through the lense of ecology and decreased dependencies to create closed and self sustaining systems. Socially I am concerned with food justice and the potential for permaculture practices in closed loop systems which plays hand in hand with ecological sustainability. During my time in school I have built a passion for computer graphics and technology to develop more efficient workflows in the architectural pipeline especially between BIM and visualization.
